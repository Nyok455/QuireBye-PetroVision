Anomaly Detection

Large daily change and high water cut are flagged automatically. Thresholds are defined in js/utils/constants.js:

ANALYTICS: { anomalyChangePct: 15, highWaterCutPct: 50 }

Decline Forecast

Field-level exponential decline is generated for 12 months by default. Adjust defaults in CONSTANTS.ANALYTICS.

  • Monthly decline default: 7%
  • Horizon default: 12 months

CSV Workflow

  1. Open Upload Data modal
  2. Download template or drag-and-drop CSV
  3. Process data to refresh KPIs, charts and table

Quick Tips
